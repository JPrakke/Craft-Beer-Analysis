What Makes These Styles Popular?

Taste varies from person to person, but what cant be argued is average and demand, and what it means for popularity. Beers produced the most, obviously sell the most and have the largest following
of fans, and highest sales. With PANDAS, we were able to find that the most popularly brewed style was IPA, or Indian Pale Ale. On top of finding out the top style of beer brewed, we can look at the
ABV of IPA, which ranges from 0.5 to 0.6, to find the most Americans prefer a beer between 0.5 and 0.6 ABV.
